the Zettelkasten Method

The Zettelkasten Method of note-taking is about connecting related notes to each other. This allows you to discover new connections and explore the network of your ideas similarly to exploring Wikipedia. For applications that support this, Zettlr is a good choice and here's a spreadsheet to compare other options.
